## Getting Started: The Vision

This book serves as a technical resource for students, developers, and enthusiasts interested in the complex interplay of **mechanical engineering, software development, and artificial intelligence** that defines modern humanoid robots.

Our goal is not just to explain concepts, but to provide a practical roadmap.

### What You Will Learn

* **Foundations of Kinematics:** Understanding how humanoid robots move and balance.
* **Full-Stack Development for Robotics:** Applying Next.js, Python, and Agentic AI to control and optimize robot behavior.
* **Actuator and Sensor Integration:** The hardware essentials for building functional robots.
* **Modern AI Integration (Agentic AI):** Implementing smart, autonomous decision-making systems.
